Coding Maniacs

Coding Maniacs is the perfect pace-based competition to improve your coding skills and test our limits. Participants have to write code solutions to multiple problem statements with real life constraints, like time complexity, space complexity along with fix input output format.

Register

Debug the Code

The Coding Contest is based on C/C++ Programming. The questions must be solved using these languages. Students from any discipline may participate and it is an individual task. It will have three stages. Codes will be evaluated on the basis of correctness of code, time complexity and space complexity.

Register

Human FoosBall

With multiplayer, 5v5 team deathmatches are at the smack dab centre of the gameplay. What makes this ridiculously fun is that everything here is fast paced — an average team match lasts for about seven minutes, so you’re not dawdling at any moment. The objective is simple — be the first to register 50 kills of your opponent team, and you win

Escape room

An escape room, also known as an escape game, puzzle room, or exit game, is a game in which a team of players discover clues, solve puzzles, and accomplish tasks in one or more rooms in order to accomplish a specific goal in a limited amount of time. The goal is often to escape from the site of the game.

ZEPHYR 2021

Ever since it was first organized in 2004, Zephyr has been the most awaited event among the students of Thakur College of Engineering and Technology. This technical festival aims at providing students with platform to enhance their technical skills. Each year with a new edition, Zephyr brings new events such as workshops on in-demand technology and skills along with gaming tournaments and fun events. This 3-day fest has been recoding and increasing footfall of Students, Teachers and Public in general. Join in Zephyr 2021 to get a glimpse of the best of TCET.
